Frequency Domain RLC Circuit Analysis

Calculate the sinusoidal steady state over a range of frequencies and find the resonance frequency of RLC (Resistor, Inductor, Capacitor) circuits.

For a detailed discussion of usage and implementation see GitHub

Here you can optionally draw the RLC circuit and the app will generate the circuit for you. Simply drag and drop components and wires onto the grid below. When dropping a component you will be prompted for its value, sources have the magnitude and phase separated by a comma. Frequency start and stop over a given number of steps can also be specified here.
